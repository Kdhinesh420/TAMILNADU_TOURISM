Velliangiri Hills, Coimbatore

Velliangiri Hills, located about 40 kilometers from Coimbatore, are a picturesque mountain range known as the 'Kailash of the South'. These beautiful hills are popular for trekking, meditation, and spiritual practices. The hills are home to several caves and meditation centers, attracting spiritual seekers from across India and the world. The trek to Velliangiri is considered a challenging but rewarding experience, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape, lush forests, and wildlife. The region is blessed with pleasant weather and fresh mountain air, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The hills have significant spiritual importance, and many ashrams and meditation centers are located here, promoting yoga and holistic wellness. The scenic beauty, combined with the spiritual atmosphere, makes Velliangiri Hills a unique destination for those seeking peace and rejuvenation.
