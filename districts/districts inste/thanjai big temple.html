Brihadeeswarar Temple, Thanjavur

The Brihadeeswarar Temple, also known as the Big Temple, is a UNESCO World Heritage site built by Raja Raja Chola I in 1010 AD. It is one of the finest examples of Tamil architecture, featuring a towering vimana (temple tower), detailed sculptures, and inscriptions showcasing the grandeur of the Chola dynasty.
