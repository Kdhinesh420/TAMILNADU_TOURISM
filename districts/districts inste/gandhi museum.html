Gandhi Memorial Museum, Madurai

The Gandhi Memorial Museum in Madurai is one of the five Gandhi Museums established across India. Located in the premises of the historic Tamukkam Palace, this museum is dedicated to the life, philosophy, and works of Mahatma Gandhi. The museum houses a collection of rare photographs, manuscripts, letters, and artifacts that chronicle Gandhi's freedom struggle and his contributions to Indian independence. Visitors can explore various exhibits showcasing different phases of Gandhi's life, his interactions with other freedom fighters, and his vision for independent India. The museum serves as an important educational institution, promoting non-violence and peace, which were central to Gandhi's ideology.
