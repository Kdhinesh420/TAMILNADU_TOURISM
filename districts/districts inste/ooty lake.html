Ooty Lake, Nilgiris

Ooty Lake is a beautiful man-made lake nestled in the picturesque hill station of Ooty. Created in 1818, this scenic water body is surrounded by lush greenery and rolling hills, making it a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. The lake is ideal for various recreational activities including boating, paddle boating, and fishing. Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks along the lake's edge, have picnics on the surrounding lawns, and capture stunning photographs of the serene landscape. The calm waters reflect the surrounding mountains and trees, creating a picture-perfect scene especially during sunrise and sunset. The area around the lake is well-maintained with gardens and pathways.
