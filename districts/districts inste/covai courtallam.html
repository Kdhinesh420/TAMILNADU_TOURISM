Covai Courtallam Waterfall, Coimbatore

Covai Courtallam is a serene and picturesque waterfall located about 35 kilometers from Coimbatore city, nestled in the midst of lush greenery and verdant forests. The waterfall cascades down the rocky terrain, creating a refreshing and rejuvenating atmosphere. The surrounding landscape is characterized by dense vegetation, towering trees, and natural pools of crystal-clear water, making it an ideal picnic spot for families and nature lovers. The cool mist from the waterfall, combined with the pleasant climate and fresh mountain air, provides a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The area around the waterfall is perfect for swimming, wading, and enjoying the natural beauty. The scenic beauty and peaceful ambiance make Covai Courtallam a favorite destination for weekend getaways and nature photography. The waterfall is particularly enchanting during the monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak.
