Meenakshi Amman Temple, Madurai

One of the most famous temples in India, the Meenakshi Amman Temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (an avatar of Parvati) and Lord Sundareswarar (an aspect of Lord Shiva). The temple is renowned for its stunning Dravidian architecture, with towering gopurams (temple towers) decorated with intricate sculptures. The temple complex houses multiple shrines, pillared halls (mandapams), and the famous Golden Lotus Pond (Golden Pond). With over 30,000 sculptures and carvings, it is one of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world.
