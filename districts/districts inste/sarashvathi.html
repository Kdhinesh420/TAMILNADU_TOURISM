Saraswathi Mahal Library, Thanjavur

The Saraswathi Mahal Library, located inside the Thanjavur Palace complex, is one of the oldest libraries in Asia. It houses over 49,000 rare manuscripts written on palm leaves and paper, covering literature, medicine, music, and astrology. Established during the Nayak rule and expanded by the Marathas, it remains a treasure trove of knowledge.
