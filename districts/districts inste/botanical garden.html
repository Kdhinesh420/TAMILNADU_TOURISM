Government Botanical Garden, Ooty

Spread over 55 acres in the scenic Nilgiri hills, the Government Botanical Garden in Ooty is a paradise for plant lovers and nature enthusiasts. Established in 1848, this magnificent garden houses thousands of exotic and indigenous plant species from around the world. The garden features carefully landscaped paths, ornamental plants, ferns, bamboo groves, medicinal herbs, and towering coniferous trees. Visitors can enjoy a serene walk through the well-maintained lawns, discover rare plant species, and experience the tranquility of nature. The garden is particularly beautiful during flowering seasons when vibrant colors blanket the landscape.
