Thiruvalluvar Statue, Kanyakumari

The Thiruvalluvar Statue is a magnificent 133-foot tall stone sculpture dedicated to Thiruvalluvar, one of Tamil literature's greatest poets and philosophers. The statue stands majestically on a rock island near the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, commanding attention from a distance with its impressive height and grandeur. Thiruvalluvar is best known for his masterpiece 'Thirukkural', a collection of 1330 moral and ethical couplets that have influenced Tamil philosophy and literature for over 2000 years. The statue showcases exquisite stone carving and artistic craftsmanship, depicting the sage in a meditative posture. Accessible by ferry, the statue site offers panoramic views of the confluence of three seas - the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean. The monument is a symbol of Tamil cultural pride and spiritual wisdom. It attracts pilgrims, scholars, and tourists interested in Tamil literature and ancient philosophy.
