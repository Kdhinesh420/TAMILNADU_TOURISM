Muttukadu Backwaters, Chengalpattu

Muttukadu Backwaters is a scenic destination famous for its beautiful backwaters, mangrove forests, and recreational water sports activities. Located on the East Coast Road (ECR) near Kovalam in Chengalpattu district, this picturesque spot offers a serene retreat away from the city's hustle and bustle. The backwaters are connected to the Bay of Bengal and provide stunning views, especially during sunrise and sunset. The surrounding mangrove forests support diverse flora and fauna, making it an ecological hotspot. Visitors can enjoy various water sports including sailing, jet skiing, kayaking, and fishing. The calm waters and scenic landscape make it ideal for picnics, photography, and leisurely boat rides. The area is well-developed with restaurants, resorts, and recreational facilities catering to tourists. Muttukadu Backwaters is perfect for families, adventure enthusiasts, and nature photographers seeking a peaceful waterside experience away from crowded beaches.
