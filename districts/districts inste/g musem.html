Government Museum, Chennai

The Government Museum, also known as the Egmore Museum, is one of the oldest museums in India. Established in 1851, it houses a rich collection of archaeology, numismatics, zoology, natural history, and contemporary art. The bronze gallery is world-famous for its Chola bronzes.
