Doddabetta Peak, Ooty

Doddabetta Peak is the highest point in the Nilgiri Mountains, standing at an elevation of 2,623 meters (8,606 feet). Located about 9 km from Ooty, this majestic peak offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, valleys, and dense forests. On clear days, visitors can see the shimmering plains in the distance. The peak is accessible by car, and a telescope is available at the top for enhanced viewing. The journey to the peak involves winding roads through tea plantations and coffee estates, adding to the scenic beauty. Sunrise and sunset at Doddabetta are particularly spectacular, attracting photographers and nature lovers from around the world. The cool climate and fresh mountain air make it a perfect destination for a peaceful getaway.
