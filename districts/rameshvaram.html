Rameshwaram District

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva, famous for its long corridors, massive sculpted pillars, and sacred water tanks.

Location: Rameshwaram Town

Timings: 5:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Pamban Bridge

A historic railway bridge that connects Rameshwaram Island with mainland India, known for its engineering marvel and scenic views.

Location: Between Rameshwaram Island and Pamban

Timings: Open 24 hours

Dhanushkodi Beach

Known as the “Ghost Town”, Dhanushkodi is a beautiful yet abandoned village at the southern tip of Rameshwaram, where the Bay of Bengal and Indian Ocean meet.

Location: 20 km from Rameshwaram

Timings: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
