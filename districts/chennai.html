Marina Beach

Marina Beach, stretching for 13 km, is the longest natural urban beach in India and the second longest in the world.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

An ancient Shiva temple located in Mylapore, Chennai, built in the 7th century CE. Famous for its towering gopuram.

Location: Mylapore, Chennai

Timings: 5:30 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Government Museum

Established in 1851, the Government Museum is the second oldest in India with a vast archaeological collection.

Location: Egmore, Chennai

Timings: 9:30 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Fridays)
