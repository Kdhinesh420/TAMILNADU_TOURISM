Tiruchirappalli (Trichy) District

Rockfort Temple

A historic fort and temple complex perched on a massive rock, offering panoramic views of the city.

Location: Trichy Town

Timings: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple (Srirangam)

One of the largest temple complexes in India, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, known for its architecture and spirituality.

Location: Srirangam, Trichy

Timings: 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Jambukeswarar Temple (Thiruvanaikaval)

A famous Shiva temple, one of the Pancha Bhoota Stalams, representing the element of water.

Location: Thiruvanaikaval, Trichy

Timings: 6:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM
