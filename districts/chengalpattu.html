Shore Temple, Mahabalipuram

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Shore Temple is one of the oldest stone temples in South India, built during the Pallava dynasty.

Location: Mahabalipuram

Timings: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Tiger Caves

An ancient rock-cut cave complex with carvings of tiger heads, located near Mahabalipuram, built during the Pallava period.

Location: Saluvankuppam, near Mahabalipuram

Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Muttukadu Backwaters

A scenic spot famous for boating and water sports, offering a peaceful retreat with beautiful backwaters and mangroves.

Location: ECR Road, near Kovalam

Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
