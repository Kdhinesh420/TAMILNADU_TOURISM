Madurai District

Meenakshi Amman Temple

One of the most famous temples in India, dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. Known for its stunning architecture and tall gopurams.

Location: Madurai City

Timings: 5:00 AM - 12:30 PM, 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace

A magnificent 17th-century palace built by King Thirumalai Nayak, showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture with huge pillars and domes.

Location: Near Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Gandhi Memorial Museum

One of the five Gandhi Museums in India, it showcases the life and works of Mahatma Gandhi with historical artifacts.

Location: Tamukkam Palace, Madurai

Timings: 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM (Closed on Fridays)
