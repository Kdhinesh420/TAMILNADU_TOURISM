Kanyakumari District

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A famous monument built in honor of Swami Vivekananda, located on a small island off the coast. Accessible by ferry, it offers stunning sea views.

Location: Off the coast of Kanyakumari

Timings: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Thiruvalluvar Statue

A 133-foot tall stone statue dedicated to the great Tamil poet and philosopher Thiruvalluvar. It stands majestically on a rock island near Vivekananda Rock.

Location: Kanyakumari Coast

Timings: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Kanyakumari Beach (Sunset Point)

Famous for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views over the confluence of the Bay of Bengal, Indian Ocean, and Arabian Sea.

Location: Kanyakumari Town

Timings: Open 24 hours
